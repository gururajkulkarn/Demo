Director of Extension

Prof. N.A. Patil, M.V.Sc., Ph.D
Director of Extension,
Karnataka Veterinary, Animal and Fisheries Sciences University, Bidar-585401

Contact
Office : 08482- 245313
Mobile: +91-9448604646, 9606038309
Email: dekvafsu@gmail.com, napvety@gmail.com

Prof. N.A. Patil has completed B.V.Sc. and M.V.Sc (Medicine) from veterinary College, UAS, Bangalore.  He has awarded PhD in Clinical Veterinary Medicine from Madras Veterinary College, Chennai.  He is the recipient of ICAR Junior Fellowship and Gold Medal during M.V.Sc. Programme.

He has 32 years of teaching research and extension experience in the field of Veterinary Science.  He has guided more than 15 post-graduate students in Veterinary Medicine.  He has published more than 75 research papers and presented more than 90 research papers in national and international conferences.  He is recipient of D.C. Blood Gold Medal by Indian society of Veterinary Medicine and several best research paper awards for research contribution presented in the conferences.

He has organised more than 25 state level training programmes.  He has given several Doordarshan and Radio programmes on Veterinary Science.  He has published 3 books and 75 popular articles in leading dailies.

He has served in the University as Associate Director of Research, Officer on Special Duty, Comptroller, Director of Instruction (PG’s), Dean and University Librarian.  He has served as member in Academic Council and Editorial Committee in scientific journal.

Fields(s) of specialization

Veterinary Medicine, Veterinary Clinical Medicin

Others Achievements

He has served about 5 years and 6 months (May 2006 to December 2011) as Veterinary Officer under Govt. of Karnataka. He has joined the university as Assistant Professor in Department of Veterinary Parasitology, Veterinary College, Shivamogga (December 2011- July 2015).  From July 2015 to July 2020 worked as Assistant professor in Veterinary College, Bidar. From July 2020 to till date, he is heading the department as Associate Professor and engaging in undergraduate Teaching, Research and Extension and He is a life member of IAAVP. He has published 12 research articles in reputed National Journals and 18 abstracts in conferences, 6 popular articles in Kannada.

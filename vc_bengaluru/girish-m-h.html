Other Achievement

Dr. Girish. M.H  Graduated from Veterinary College, Bidar (UAS, Dharwad) in the year 2003 and did his Post Graduation from Bangalore Veterinary College (UAS Bangalore), in the year 2005. Later he joined as Veterinary Officer in the Dept of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Services Govt of Karnataka and served in mysore for more than six years. In 2012 Joined KVAFSU, Bidar as a Assistant Professor in the Dept of Anatomy veterinary College, Bidar.  He is involved in UG/PG teaching. Published 20 research papers and abstracts and received Best paper award at IAVA conference held at Jabalpur in 2007. Presently working as Assistant professor in the Department of Anatomy.

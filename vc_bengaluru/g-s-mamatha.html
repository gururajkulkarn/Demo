Other Achievements

Working as Assistant Professor since 2012. Offered General Parasitology and Veterinary Helminthology, Veterinary Entomology and Acarology and Veterinary Protozoology courses to undergraduate students.  Offered prescribed postgraduate courses to masters and Ph.D students. Guided postgraduate students in Veterinary Parasitology (2 MVSc) and served as a member of Advisory Committee for 2 Ph. D and  7 seven MVSc students.

Identified and reported Theileria luwenshuni for the first time in India by PCR in both sheep and goats causing theleriosis.

Involved in conducting 21 days National Training Programme to teachers/ scientists under Centre of Advanced Faculty Training sponsored by ICAR, New Delhi.

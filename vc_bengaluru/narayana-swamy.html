Other achievement

The five books published by Prof. M.Narayana Swamy in Kannada are  1) ‘Jaanuvaaru’ (livestock) by Kannada Pustaka Pradhikara, Government of Karnataka, 2). ‘Tenemareya Kranthi Ragi Lakshmanaiah’ (the collection of articles written by 24 writers on renowned Ragi breeder Dr. C. H. Lakshmanaiah who developed high yielding INDAF Ragi varieties), 3). ‘Pashuvaidya Sampada’ (the collection of extension articles on Veterinary Science, Animal Husbandry and General Science published by him and co-authors in leading daily Kannada news paper such as Prajavani and other periodicals), 4). ‘Nimage Neeve Belakaagi’ and 5). ‘Mahanadiya Ugama’.

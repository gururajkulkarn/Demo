Proposed research programmes

Project 1. Research Projects proposed for 2019-20

1

Title of the project

“Utilization of farm ponds for enhancing fish production in selected districts of Northern Karnataka using improved Amur common carp”

2

Name of PI & Co-PIs

 Dr. Vijaykumar.S, Dr Vijay Atnur, Dr Muttappa Khavi FRIC,Vijayapur

3

Objectives

  1. A participatory action research project will be conducted applying a variety of experimental combination ( Monoculture, Polyculture, Integrated fish culture with poultry, cattle, horticulture, duck etc),potential of farm ponds to increase relative productivity and profitability of fish culture in Vijayapur.
  2. The overall objective of the project is to develop, test and demonstrate strategies, methods and techniques for assisting rural people in improving their quality of life through the development of aquaculture in farm ponds.

4

Technical Programme

1. In rural areas of Vijayapur, the vast majority of households own small farm ponds and the pond dikes are covered with large timber trees,a situation where it is quite difficult to produce fish and vegetable on the dikes. Incorporation of appropriate technologies however, can successfully utilize farm ponds for commercial fish farming that can meet growing protein and nutritional demand.
2.Growth rate, productivity and economic returns from fish farming with Indian major carps and Amur common carp will be found by adopting different methods of fish culture like monoculture and polyculture in farm ponds without Polyethene sheet, farm pond with Polyethene sheet and soil bed.
3.Optimize stocking density of fish in farm ponds over a wide range of locations improve overall production of fish in farm ponds
4. Prepare a standard package of practice for fish culture in farm ponds where water exchange is more than 30% per day.
5. Awareness of fish farming in farm ponds through demonstration programme helps in attraction of farmers for aqualture and improves their socio economic status.

5

Duration

3 years

6

Budget proposed (Rs.)

29.95 Lakhs.

7

Funding agency

NFDB, Hyderabad

Project 3. Research Projects proposed for 2019-20

1

Title of the project

“Conservation and Propagation of Indigenous Fish Species occurred in North part of Karnataka under center of excellence  at FRIC, Bhutnal, Vijayapur”

2

Name of PI & Co-PIs

 Dr. Vijaykumar.S, Dr Vijay S. Atnur, Dr Muttappa Khavi FRIC,Vijayapur

3

Objectives

1.Identification, conservation and propagation of indigenous species
2. Brood stock management: Artificial breeding of threatened species of restocking in their natural habitat and to establish gene banks using cryopreservation techniques.
3. To overcome disease problems in larval rearing tanks and culture ponds.
4. To generate income, self employment and skill for interested farmers.
5. To maintain museum of indigenous species.

4

Technical Programme

A.There are community conservation initiatives which protect not only rare native fish species, but the entire riverine habitats through simple, participatory measures, without any infrastructure development are external funding.
B. The conservation of natural aquatic ecosystem with many species of fishes and other aquatic flora and fauna need to be facilitate.
C. The exotic fishes have also been introduced in the water bodies tends to create their own ecologies and some of the issues involved with them need to be addressed

5

Duration

5 years

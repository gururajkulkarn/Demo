OthersAchievements

I started my career working at Department of Poultry Science, Veterinary College, Bangalore as JRF/SRF under research projects funded by DBT, ICAR and Industries. The main area of research was on Mycotoxins and Enzymes in poultry diets. The research findings were Presented at 23rd IPSACON,held at Hyderabad from 2-4, Februaty,2005 and at World's Poultry Congress (WPC), June 8- 1 4, 2004, Istanbul, TURKEY. Joined Biocon, Bangalore, as Senior marketing Executive travelled throughout the country and got a good exposure of the Poultry Industry. Joined UAS, Bangalore as Subject Matter Specialist (Animal Science) atKVK, Chamarajanagara and I was instrumental in developing the animal science activities in the KVK by establishing all the demonstration units. Conducted frontline demonstrations, on farm testing's, on and off campus trainings for the farmers, women entrepreneurs, unemployed youth and for the extension functionaries in various animal husbandry activities. After joining to Veterinary College Shivamogga, involved in UG, PG teaching, extensionactivities and research activities.Establishment of Department of livestock production management as per VCI standards.Trained the farmers and farm women in various aspects of poultry production to become entrepreneurs. Operated RKVY and Sujala projects, atomizationof poultry sheds.

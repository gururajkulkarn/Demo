Others Achievements

Dr. M.K. Tandle,is working as Director of Instruction  (Post Graduate Studies) of Karnataka Veterinary, Animal and Fisheries Sciences University, Bidar and he has assumed the office on 1.6.2019 for a tenure of 4 years. He has worked in different capacities like Veterinary Officer, Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, Warden, Chief Warden, Chairman, Tender Processing Cell and Professor and Head in Department of Gynaecology & Obstetrics of Veterinary College, Bidar, Karnataka Veterinary, Animal and Fisheries Sciences University, Bidar. Guided 15 MVSc students, Advisory committee members for more than 30 MVSc and 2 PhD students. As Course Director for Refresher Training Course on Artificial insemination and treatment of infertility in dairy cattle trained more than 550 Veterinary officers. Edited various books like Reproductive disorders of livestock edited by Suresh S Honnappagol, MK Tandle, S D Sonwane and R G Bijurkar, Agrotech Publishing Academy, Udaipur, year 2006, pages 320. Veterinary Obstetrics- A Practical Guide edited by Suresh S Honnappagol, MK Tandle, Jaypee Brothers Medical Publishers, New Delhi, year 2009, Pages 164, Veterinary, Animal and Poultry Science Dictionary, edited by Suresh S Honnappagol, MK Tandle Jaypee Brothers Medical Publishers, New Delhi, year 2010, pages 174, Artificial Insemination and treatment of infertility in dairy animals, edited by Suresh S Honnappagol, MK Tandle New India Publishing Agency, New Delhi, year 2010, pages 150, A Practical manual of Veterinary Obstetrics by Khaja Mohteshamuddin, M K Tandle and R G Bijurkar by Satish Serial Publishing House, Delhi, Year 2015, Pages 72, A Practical manual of Veterinary Andrology and Reproductive Techniques by Khaja Mohteshamuddin, M K Tandle and R G Bijurkar by Satish Serial Publishing House, Delhi, Year 2015, Pages 69, A Practical manual of Veterinary Gynaecology by Khaja Mohteshamuddin, M K Tandle and R G Bijurkar by Satish Serial Publishing House, Delhi, Year 2015, Pages 99, Veterinary Andrology & Artificial Insemination in Dairy Animals by New India Publishing Agency, New Delhi, year 2017, pages 246
